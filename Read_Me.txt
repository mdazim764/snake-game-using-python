tips for Play Snake Game..

1) Press Up Arrow key to move snake Up
2) Press Down Arrow key to move snake Down
3) Press Left Arrow key to move snake Left
4) Press Right Arrow key to move snake Right
5) Press S key to Stop the Snake


** Expert Tips...

1) Press Q key to Increase Score in (2)
2) Press f key to Increase Snake Speed..
3) Press l key to Decrease Snake Speed..
